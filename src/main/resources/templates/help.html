FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I create a store?

After registering, go to your Dashboard and click "Create Store". Fill in your store name, upload a logo and banner, then submit.

How do I subscribe to a plan?

Navigate to the Subscription page from your dashboard. Choose a plan that fits your needs and complete the payment to activate your store.

How many products can I upload?

The number of products depends on your subscription plan. Upgrade your plan to increase your product limit.

Is it free to register?

Yes! Registration is completely free. You only pay when you choose a subscription plan to start listing products.
